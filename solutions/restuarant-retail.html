The Real Problem

Why great food alone
isn't enough anymore

Customers Google before they visit

Before anyone walks through your door, they've already Googled you, looked at your menu, checked your photos, and read your reviews. If your site doesn't impress, they book elsewhere.

Your menu is hard to find or read

A PDF menu that won't load on mobile, or a menu buried three clicks deep, loses customers before they've even seen what you offer. Menus need to be front, fast, and beautiful.

No way to take bookings online

People want to book a table at 11pm without calling anyone. If you don't have an online reservation system, you're handing those bookings to competitors who do.

Bad photos or no atmosphere online

People eat with their eyes first. A website with bad or no food photography fails to create the hunger and desire that actually drives bookings. We help you showcase your food the right way.

Common Questions

Questions from
restaurant owners

Can you integrate with OpenTable or Resy?
Yes. We integrate with OpenTable, Resy, SevenRooms, and also direct booking forms. If you want to avoid commission fees entirely, we can build a direct form that sends bookings straight to your email or phone.
Can I update the menu myself?
Menu changes are included in your monthly plan — just message us with the updates and we'll have them live within 24 hours. Seasonal menus, price changes, new dishes, removed items — all handled at no extra cost.
Do you help with food photography?
We don't shoot photography ourselves, but we design the site to showcase whatever photos you provide brilliantly. If your photos need improvement, we can recommend local food photographers and advise on what shots will work best on the site.
Can you set up online ordering without losing margin to delivery apps?
Yes — our Scale plan includes direct online ordering via Stripe. Customers order through your own website, you keep 100% of the revenue, and payments land directly in your bank account with no commission to third parties.
What if I have multiple locations?
Multi-location sites are available on our Scale plan. Each location gets its own page with its own menu, hours, and booking system. Contact us and we'll scope the right solution for your group.
