Common Questions

Questions from
local business owners

Do I need a website if I already have a Google Business profile?
Yes. Your Google Business profile gets you found — your website closes the deal. Customers click through to check credibility, see your work, and decide whether to call. A profile alone isn't enough to convert a serious customer.
How do you handle the SEO for local searches?
We build dedicated service area pages (e.g. "Electrician in Springfield") with proper schema markup, Google Maps embedding, and keyword-optimised content. This is the foundation of ranking for local intent searches.
Can I get quote requests sent directly to my phone?
Yes. We connect your contact form to email and can also hook it up to SMS or WhatsApp so you get notified instantly whenever someone submits a quote request — day or night.
What if I want to add new services or change my service area?
Just message us. All content changes — new services, updated pricing, expanded service areas, new photos — are included in your monthly plan and done within 24 business hours.
I'm not technical at all. Does that matter?
Not at all. You never need to touch code, a CMS, or any technical system. You send us a message — we handle everything on the back end. That's the whole point of Sitezy.
