FAQ

Questions from
healthcare clients

Can you connect to our booking software?
Yes — we can embed booking widgets from most popular healthcare booking platforms (Zocdoc, Jane App, Cliniko, etc.) or use smart enquiry forms that send appointment requests directly to your inbox. Mention your booking system in the brief.
Is the site HIPAA-compliant?
Our websites do not store patient health information — we use enquiry forms that collect only basic contact details. For full HIPAA-compliant patient portals or EHR integration, we recommend using your dedicated practice management software for those functions.
How do you handle content updates when our services change?
Simply email us the changes — new services, updated team members, changed hours, new photos — and we apply them within 24 hours as part of your unlimited edits. No login or technical knowledge needed.
