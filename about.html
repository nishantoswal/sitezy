We spent years working in and around web design agencies. We saw the inside of the machine — the bloated proposals, the six-figure quotes for five-page websites, the three-month timelines that quietly became six, the developers who disappeared after launch leaving business owners with a site they couldn't update.

And on the other side of that table, we met the people on the receiving end. The plumber who paid $8,000 for a site that never ranked. The restaurant owner who waited four months for their launch and missed an entire busy season. The consultant who had to pay $150 every time they needed to change a phone number on their homepage.

"The problem wasn't that agencies did bad work. The problem was that their model was built for enterprises — and small businesses were paying enterprise prices for it."

So we built a different model. One where you see the finished website before spending a single dollar. Where updates happen within 24 hours — not after a support ticket queue. Where the monthly fee covers everything: design, hosting, SSL, maintenance, edits, support. No surprise invoices. No lock-in. No disappearing developers.

We called it Sitezy. The idea was straightforward: make a professional web presence accessible to every business, at a price that makes sense. The web, simplified.

Since launching, we've helped businesses across 7 countries go from no online presence to consistent inbound leads, bookings, and revenue through their websites. We are proud of that. And we are just getting started.
