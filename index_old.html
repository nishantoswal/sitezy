Questions Answered

Everything you
need to know

How does the free preview work?+
Fill out our 2-minute brief — no technical knowledge needed. Our design team builds a real, working preview of your site within 48 hours. We schedule a 20-minute walkthrough call to review it and you can request changes. You only subscribe if you love it. No credit card needed until you decide to go live.
What's included in "unlimited edits"?+
Unlimited edits covers all text updates, image swaps, pricing changes, adding/removing service items, updating hours or contact info, and minor layout adjustments. For major redesigns we'll scope separately — but day-to-day updates are always unlimited and completed within 24 business hours.
Do I own my website or is it locked to Sitezy?+
You own your domain name and all your content — always. If you ever decide to leave, we provide a full export of your website files. Your data is never held hostage. Your domain stays yours regardless of your Sitezy subscription.
How long does it take to go fully live?+
Most sites go fully live within 5–7 business days from brief submission. This includes: 48 hours for your initial preview, any revisions (1–2 rounds typically), domain connection, email setup, and final launch checks.
Can I upgrade or downgrade my plan later?+
Yes. Upgrade at any time and we'll immediately expand your site. Downgrading is also possible — give us 30 days notice and we'll adjust at the next billing cycle. No upgrade fees or penalties, ever.
What if I don't like the preview?+
No problem — you walk away at zero cost. Our preview is completely free with no strings attached. We'll offer one round of revisions to get it right. If it's still not right, you owe us nothing.
Do you build e-commerce sites?+
Yes. Our Scale plan includes full e-commerce capability, integrating Shopify, WooCommerce, or Stripe. Product pages, shopping cart, checkout, payment processing — all handled.
Is there a minimum contract?+
No minimum contract. Sitezy is month-to-month by default. Cancel any time from your dashboard with no fees, no lock-in, no penalties.
