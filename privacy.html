Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters to us. This policy explains what data we collect, why, and how we protect it. We will never sell your personal information — ever.

1. Who This Applies To

This policy applies to all visitors of sitezy.co and all subscribers to Sitezy services. "You" means any individual accessing our site or using our services.

2. What We Collect

  • Information you provide: Name, business name, email, and phone number when submitting a preview request or contact form.
  • Payment data: Processed securely via Stripe. Sitezy never stores card details — Stripe handles all payment data under PCI-DSS compliance.
  • Usage data: Pages visited, time on site, device type, and referral source — collected anonymously via Google Analytics.
  • Communications: Emails and messages you send us, retained to provide support and maintain service history.

3. How We Use It

  • To build and deliver your website preview and live site
  • To process subscription payments
  • To respond to support requests and edit submissions
  • To send service communications (invoices, notices)
  • To understand how our site is used and improve it

We do not use your data for advertising profiling or sell it to any third party.

4. Who We Share Data With

Only with service providers essential to running Sitezy:

  • Stripe — payment processing
  • Formspree — form submission handling
  • Google Analytics — anonymous usage analytics
  • Cloudflare / CDN providers — website hosting and delivery

All providers are contractually bound to protect your data and cannot use it for their own purposes.

5. Cookies

We use cookies for analytics (Google Analytics) and to remember preferences. You can disable cookies in your browser settings without affecting your ability to use the site.

6. Data Retention

We retain personal data for as long as your account is active and for a reasonable period after closure for legal obligations. Analytics data is retained for 26 months. You may request deletion at any time.

7. Your Rights

You may have the right to: access data we hold about you; request correction of inaccurate data; request deletion; object to processing; request a portable copy. Email hello@sitezy.co to exercise any right. We respond within 30 days.

8. Security

We use SSL encryption, access controls, and regular security reviews. No system is fully immune — if you believe your data is compromised, contact us immediately.

9. Children

Sitezy is not directed at individuals under 16. We do not knowingly collect data from minors. Contact us if you believe we have, and we will delete it promptly.

10. Changes

Material changes to this policy will be communicated by email. Continued use after changes take effect constitutes acceptance.

Privacy questions or requests?

Email hello@sitezy.co — we respond to all privacy requests within 30 days.
