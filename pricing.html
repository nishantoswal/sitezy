Pricing Questions

Everything you need
to know about pricing

Is there really a $0 setup fee?
Yes. We don't charge a setup fee at any plan level. Your first payment is your first monthly subscription — and only after you've approved the preview. We build your preview completely free.
What counts as an "unlimited edit"?
Text changes, image swaps, pricing updates, new service items, contact info, hours, seasonal offers — all covered within 24 business hours. Full page redesigns are scoped separately but rarely needed.
Can I cancel at any time?
Yes. No contracts, no cancellation fees, no lock-in. We ask for 30 days notice so we can provide a clean offboarding — including a full export of your website files.
What does the annual plan include?
Annual billing gives you 10% off your monthly rate. Everything else is identical — same features, same support, same cancel-anytime policy. You pay annually upfront and save two months over the year.
Can I change plans later?
Absolutely. Upgrade at any time and we expand your site immediately. Downgrade by giving 30 days notice and we adjust at the next billing cycle. No fees or penalties either way.
