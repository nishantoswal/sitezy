Questions Answered

Everything you
need to know

How does the free preview work?
Fill out our 2-minute brief — no technical knowledge needed. Our design team builds a real, working preview within 48 hours. We walk you through it on a 20-min call and you can request any changes. You only subscribe if you love it. No credit card needed until you go live.
What's included in "unlimited edits"?
Text updates, image swaps, pricing changes, adding/removing service items, updating hours or contact info, and minor layout adjustments. Major redesigns are scoped separately — but day-to-day updates are always unlimited and completed within 24 business hours.
Do I own my website or is it locked to Sitezy?
You own your domain name and all your content — always. If you ever decide to leave, we provide a full export of your website files. Your data is never held hostage.
How long does it take to go fully live?
Most sites go live within 5–7 business days from brief submission — including: 48 hours for your initial preview, 1–2 revision rounds, domain connection, email setup, and final launch checks.
Can I upgrade or downgrade my plan?
Yes. Upgrade at any time and we expand your site immediately. Downgrading is possible with 30 days notice. No fees or penalties, ever.
What if I don't like the preview?
You walk away at zero cost. We offer one revision round to get it right. If it's still not right, you owe us nothing.
Do you build e-commerce sites?
Yes. Our Scale plan includes full e-commerce via Shopify, WooCommerce, or Stripe. Product pages, cart, checkout, payments — all handled. Contact us for large catalogues and we'll scope a custom solution.
Is there a minimum contract?
No minimum contract. Month-to-month by default. Cancel any time from your dashboard — no fees, no lock-in.
